Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025
At Arya Flow, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website or yoga consultation services.

1 Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and preferences when you fill out our consultation or registration forms.

What We Collect: Personal information provided voluntarily through our website forms, registration processes, and consultation bookings.

2 How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide you with the best possible service and enhance your experience with Aryaflow. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • To schedule yoga consultations and respond to your inquiries.
  • To send class details, reminders, and updates related to your sessions.
  • To improve our services and website experience.

We are committed to using your information solely for the purposes mentioned above and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

3 Data Protection

Your personal data is kept confidential and secure. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4 Email Communication

By submitting your details, you consent to receive communication related to Arya Flow's yoga programs. You can unsubscribe anytime by contacting us at aryaflowofficial@gmail.com.

Our email communications may include:

  • Confirmation of bookings and consultations
  • Class schedules and updates
  • Promotional offers and wellness tips
  • Important service announcements

You have the right to opt-out of marketing communications at any time while still receiving essential service-related emails.

5 Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party tools (like Formspree) to handle form submissions. These tools have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them.

Third-Party Processors: We carefully select third-party service providers that comply with data protection regulations and maintain high security standards.

We do not control the privacy practices of these third-party services and are not responsible for their privacy policies or practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services before providing your personal information.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To do so, please contact us directly.

Your data protection rights include:

  • Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another organization
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at aryaflowofficial@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7 Updates to This Policy

Arya Flow reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Stay Informed: We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Significant changes to this policy will be communicated to you via email or through a prominent notice on our website.

8 Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal data, please don't hesitate to contact us:

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.
